Description


Chicken Curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with chicken pieces cooked in a spiced sauce. It is a popular dish in many cultures, especially in Indian and Southeast Asian cuisines. The dish is typically served with rice or bread, making it a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 large onions, diced
  • 1/3 cup curry powder, or to taste
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat until hot. Add onion and saut√© until soft and golden brown, 5 to 8 minutes.
  3. Add chicken; reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 20 to 40 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  4. Uncover and cook, stirring constantly to prevent burning, until pan juices reduce to desired amount, 3 to 5 minutes.
